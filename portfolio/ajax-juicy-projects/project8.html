Voting Initiative
Social and web campaign to promote voter turnout.

Overview: Initiative focused on the importance of voting on Election Day. The intent of the campaign was to get voters to the polls.

Features: Responsive website with embedded social widget, meme generator, media coverage, newsletter sign-up, and voting registration.

Metrics: Campaign was featured on NBC's "Today" show, Times Square billboard, and celebrity ensorsements such as Kendall Jenner, Jesse Jackson, Jim Parsons, Jimmy Kimmel, and more.
