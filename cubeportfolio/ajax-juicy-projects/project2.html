Storytellers
Live storytelling from local community

Overview: The Storytellers Project website allows users to purchase tickets to live events, experience past events through photo galleries, as well as audio and video of the individual local speakers that shared their stories.

Features: Responsive national and site built in Node.js and Python that allows users to purchase tickets for upcoming events and relive past events via audio, video and photo galleries all within their city; become a speaker at the event through form sign-up; reach out to local sales reps for sponsorships; plus, all content powered by custom built content management system so local markets can manage. In addition, microservices were built for video, photo galleries and Instagram.

Metrics: To be rolled out to 20+ markets by end of Q1, 2017.
