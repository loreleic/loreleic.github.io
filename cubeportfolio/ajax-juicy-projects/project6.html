Social Issues Series
Events series and later profiles of Americans that highlights the issues that divide or unite the country.

Overview: Responsive website that for version 1 focused on an event series: live town hall discussions in 10 key Gannett markets with content and assets shared across the USA TODAY Media Network; while for version 2 was redesigned as a weekly series of profiles on Americans and their stance on issues that unite or divide the U.S.

Features: V1 featured newsletter sign-up, event schedule, news coverage from the events; while V2 was built in Wordpress with Formstack and Brightcove video integration.
