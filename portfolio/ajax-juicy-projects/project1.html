Best Of
Readers vote on their city's best things to do

Overview: “Best Of” is a compilation of the best local things to do, businesses, people, and places in a city as chosen by users.

Features: Responsive website built in Node.js and Python that allows for custom design elements, sort/filter function, image/map/video support, subscriber access integration, and powered by custom built content management system.

Metrics: Generated more than $1.3M in revenue within 1 year for 12 markets; 200K - 1M page views for smaller markets to larger markets; and avg. time on site ranging from 3-9 minutes per visit.
